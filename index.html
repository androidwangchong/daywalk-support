Daywalk Support

Daywalk is a simple app designed for writing one line per day. All data is stored locally on your device.

Why can I only write one line?

The limitation is intentional. Daywalk is built around constraint.

Can I edit or recover entries?

No. Once an entry is saved, it cannot be edited or filled in later.

Is my data backed up?

Daywalk does not use cloud sync. Your data remains on your device.
